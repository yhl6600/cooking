Recipe

Crust
0. leave ingredients in room temperature for 30 min before start
1. crush butter, flour, and sugar
2. push and spread in the pan
3. bake 15 min in 200C

Cream
1. mix cream, cream cheese, sugar for 5 min.
2. add eggs and mix them
3. pour on the baked crust 
4. bake 30 min in 150C and 20 min in 180C
5. cool down overnight and put it in refrigerator
